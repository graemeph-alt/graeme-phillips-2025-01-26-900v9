Our Story

Founded in 2012, our journey began when I discovered the transformative power of astrophotography during a chance encounter with a meteor shower in the Australian outback. What started as a personal passion project has evolved into a professional pursuit of capturing the universe's most spectacular phenomena, from distant galaxies to nebulous star-forming regions.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between Earth and the cosmos, making the wonders of space accessible to everyone through stunning imagery. We believe in the power of astrophotography to inspire scientific curiosity, environmental awareness, and a deeper appreciation for our place in the universe. Our core values of innovation, precision, and artistic excellence guide every project we undertake.

Frequently Asked Questions

What equipment do you use for your astrophotography?

I use professional-grade telescopes, including a 14" Celestron EdgeHD and various specialized cameras optimized for deep-sky imaging. Each project may require different equipment based on the specific celestial object and desired outcome.

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects take 2-4 weeks from initial capture to final processing. Complex deep-sky objects may require multiple imaging sessions to capture all necessary details.

Do you offer workshops or training sessions?

Yes, I regularly conduct both online and in-person workshops for beginners and advanced astrophotographers. These sessions cover equipment selection, imaging techniques, and post-processing methods.

What print options are available?

I offer various high-quality print options including metal prints, acrylic mounts, and traditional fine art papers. Each medium is carefully selected to enhance the specific characteristics of each image.

Can you capture specific celestial events or objects?

Yes, I accept custom commissions for specific celestial objects or events. Planning is essential as some objects are only visible during certain seasons or require specific atmospheric conditions.
